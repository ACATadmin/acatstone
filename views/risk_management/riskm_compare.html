Risk Differentiator

Notes

The application utilizes the latest ACAT analysis engine technology to effectively track the historical data of the selected currencies in real time, along with other multiple factors such as: volatility, daily movement averages, statistical factors of gains and losses over a few consecutive days, maximum drawdown, VAR and other various risk correlation factors. The application effectively implements big-data, multi-dimensional, analysis calculations to obtain the various risk indicators.

FAQ

  • Q: Why do I need to choose two cryptocurrencies for risk assessment?

    A: Risk comparison makes it more intuitive to understand the risk level.

  • Q: How many cryptocurrencies are supported?

    A: As of its current version, the application can support the evaluation of 50 mainstream cryptocurrencies.

  • Q: Is there a difference between the risk assessment reports for different currencies?

    A: The relevant risk indicators will be obtained based on the situation of the two selected cryptocurrencies in real-time, thus different risk assessment reports will be produced according to risk differences between the currencies chosen.
