The TOP10 Cryptocurrency Index Application is an application based on the ACAT Index Engine that reflects the best currency price trends in the digital currency market. Users can use this application to effectively understand the current state of the digital currency market at anytime and anywhere. It is also possible to compare a single currency that has been followed with an index at any time to determine the competitive relationship of a single currency relative to an index price trend. In the future, users can directly purchase financial derivatives related to the index to get the best investment experience.
