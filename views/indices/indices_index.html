FAQ

  • Q: What is the standard currency unit of the index?

    A: All prices, (including market capitalization and transaction amounts), of the index are denominated in US dollars (USD), and are set in the UTC time zone.

  • Q: How long is the index’s adjustment period?

    A: The index component currency is adjusted to be recalculated every 30 days, and the currencies in the sample pool are calculated in real time.

  • Q: Why is the adjustment cycle of the constituent currency carried out monthly?

    A: Different from the quarterly adjustment index of the traditional financial market, the application has adopted monthly adjustments, mainly to meet the fluctuations of the digital currency market and other large changes. Due to market volatility, the previous component currency may have a shrinking market value and is not representative of the broader market. There is also a new currency market value that rises to become a representative currency, so it needs to be adjusted regularly.
    However, if the adjustment period is too frequent (for example, requires daily adjustments), the component currency is unstable, which makes some users who want to operate according to the index component confused, as such, the adjustment period is set to one month.
