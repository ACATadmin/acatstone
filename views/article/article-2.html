Technical Analysis

Technical analysis means calculating a series of technical indicators based on the historical data of a given cryptocurrency, primarily its price and volume, and judging market conditions such as trends and vibration and reversion, and providing trading signals at specific times like: buying, selling, cutting loss or adding a position. Although technical analysis provides trading signals, it does not make strict probabilistic forecasting as market forecasting does.
