Multi Data

Multidimensional data mainly makes use of a big data technique where huge amounts of data are extracted from multiple sources related to the blockchain industry. It does this in a quick and precise way, and enables multidimensional, systematic ordering and analysis of the blockchain industry for users. Compared to other sections, the multidimensional data section is mainly focused on large volume extraction and the different aspects of data ordering, where most of the data is extracted from the markets rather than the financial data itself.
